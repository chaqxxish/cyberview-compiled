Consultancy Services

An Insider's
Perspective

Enjoy our range of services that include consultancy services for government and technology park managers to find ways of creating socio-economic wealth.

Be it with property developers, local authorities, or contractors; our consultancy service allows clients to have one main liaison to see their projects evolve from blueprint to a successful end, with certainty, expertise and very little hassle.
Divided in three main stages, the consultancy we offer depends on the readiness of your location.

Assessment Stage

Stage 1 : Getting Started

So you are about to get started? We're here to help. Our services at this stage focus on situational assessment to produce the recommendation components be it physical or soft infrastructure.

  1. Market study & site assessment
  2. Data analytics and deduction
  3. Fixing priorities
  4. Due diligence & feasibility study
  5. Project budgeting
