Career Details

Senior Executive, Financial Planning & Reporting

  • Dec 16, 2015

  • Experience Level : 5 - 6 Years

  • Employment Type : Full Time

Job Purpose

Incumbent must ensure delivery and accuracy of monthly management accounts and prepare draft financial statements for the company.

Job Deliverables
  • Assist in external audit process to ensure timely resolution of audit issues and completion of audit
  • Prepare audit schedules and liaise with external auditors on statutory audit
  • Prepare draft financial statements for manager’s review and finalisation
  • Support in annual budget preparation
  • Ensure all transactions are properly recorded and entered into the accounting system
  • Prepare monthly management accounts in compliance with the approved accounting standards
  • Assist in review and resolve all issues relating to month end closing accounts
  • Administrator for the accounting system (GL/COA)
Job Requirements
  • Candidate must possess at least a Bachelor's Degree, Professional Degree in Finance/Accountancy/Banking or equivalent with a CGPA of 3.00 and above
  • At least 5 -6 year(s) of working experience in the related field is required for this position
  • Have a good knowledge of management accounting and good understanding of the concepts
  • Experience working in audit firm will be an added advantage
