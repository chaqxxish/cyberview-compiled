Capital that Delivers

Being a home to 800 tech companies and more than 38,000 knowledge workers, we take pride in managing The Capital that is thriving with work and activities. It is our duty to make sure everything runs efficiently and liveability is at an optimal level.

This among others is what makes Cyberjaya a strong Global Tech Hub.

Township Management

As a recognition of our effort in driving investments into Cyberjaya and assisting the investors and stakeholders, we were appointed the Cybercity Manager for Cyberjaya by the Ministry of Science and Technology (MOSTI) in 2011. As the Cybercity Manager, we were mandated to be the main reference point for the stakeholders. Whether you’ve been here for a while or just moving in, talk to us for any Cyberjaya-specific information, updates, business matching and opportunities that The Capital has to offer.

A global community is a connected community. Nobody understands this better than we do. Hence why we are leading the Wireless Cyberjaya project to provide free wifi city-wide at an impressive speed. The project is developed in phases with phase one covering 19 locations. If you’re in Cyberjaya, get connected through Wireless Cyberjaya.
